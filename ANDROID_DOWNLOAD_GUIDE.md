# Android Download & Access Guide

## How to Access the Production System Apps on Your Android Phone

The Production System consists of three web applications that you can access directly from your Android phone's browser. No app store download required!

---

## Quick Start - 3 Simple Steps

### Step 1: Open the App in Chrome
1. Open **Google Chrome** on your Android phone
2. Enter the app URL in the address bar:
   - **Production App**: `https://your-production-app.netlify.app`
   - **Packing App**: `https://your-packing-app.netlify.app`
   - **Inventory App**: `https://your-inventory-app.netlify.app`

   > **Note**: Ask your supervisor for the exact URLs if you don't have them

### Step 2: Sign In (if required)
- **Production App & Packing App**: Tap "Sign in with Google" and use your work Google account
- **Inventory App**: No sign-in required (read-only access)

### Step 3: Add to Home Screen (Optional - Recommended)
This makes the app work like a real Android app:
1. Tap the **3-dot menu** (⋮) in the top-right corner of Chrome
2. Select **"Add to Home screen"**
3. Enter a name (e.g., "Production App")
4. Tap **"Add"**
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen like a regular app!

## Detailed Instructions

### Method 1: Using Chrome Browser (Recommended)

#### Why Chrome?
- Best compatibility with web apps
- Supports "Add to Home Screen" feature
- Works with Google OAuth sign-in

#### Steps:
1. **Open Chrome**
   - Locate the Chrome app on your phone (blue, red, yellow, green circle icon)
   - If you don't have Chrome, download it from Google Play Store

2. **Navigate to the App**
   - Tap the address bar at the top
   - Type or paste the app URL
   - Tap **Go** or press Enter

3. **Sign In (Production & Packing Apps)**
   - You'll see a "Sign in with Google" button
   - Tap the button
   - Select your work Google account
   - Tap "Allow" to grant permissions
   - You're in!

4. **Bookmark for Easy Access** (Optional)
   - Tap the **⭐ star icon** in the address bar
   - Choose a folder (or create "Work Apps" folder)
   - Tap "Done"
   - Access later via Chrome menu → Bookmarks

### Method 2: Add to Home Screen (App-Like Experience)

This creates a shortcut on your home screen that opens the app in full-screen mode, making it feel like a native Android app.

#### Steps:

1. **Open the app in Chrome** (follow Method 1 steps 1-2)

2. **Access Chrome Menu**
   - Tap the **3-dot menu** (⋮) in the top-right corner

3. **Select "Add to Home screen"**
   - Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home screen"**
   - You may see different options depending on your Android version:
     - "Add to Home screen"
     - "Install app"
     - "Create shortcut"

4. **Name the Shortcut**
   - A popup appears with a suggested name
   - Edit the name if you want (e.g., "Packing", "Production", "Inventory")
   - Tap **"Add"**

5. **Confirm Placement**
   - Android may ask where to place the icon
   - Choose your home screen or a specific folder
   - Tap **"Add automatically"** or **"Add to Home screen"**

6. **Done!**
   - The app icon now appears on your home screen
   - Tap the icon to open the app directly (no browser UI visible)
   - It will open in full-screen mode like a real app

#### Benefits of "Add to Home Screen":
- **Faster access**: One tap to open
- **Full screen**: No browser address bar or buttons
- **App-like feel**: Looks and works like a native Android app
- **Offline capability**: Some features may work offline (if supported)

### Method 3: Using Other Browsers

If you prefer other browsers, you can still access the apps:

#### Samsung Internet Browser
1. Open Samsung Internet
2. Navigate to the app URL
3. Tap the **menu** (≡)
4. Select **"Add page to" → Home screen**

#### Firefox
1. Open Firefox
2. Navigate to the app URL
3. Tap the **3-dot menu** (⋮)
4. Select **"Install"** or **"Add to Home screen"**

## Troubleshooting

### "Cannot open page" or "Connection error"
**Solution**:
- Check your internet connection (WiFi or mobile data)
- Make sure you have the correct URL
- Try refreshing the page (swipe down from top)
- Ask your supervisor for the correct app URL

### "Failed to sign in" or "OAuth error"
**Solution**:
- Make sure you're using **Google Chrome**
- Use your **work Google account** (not personal)
- Clear Chrome cache:
  1. Chrome menu → Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data
  2. Select "Cookies and site data" and "Cached images and files"
  3. Tap **Clear data**
- Try signing in again

### "Access denied" or "403 error"
**Solution**:
- Your Google account may not have permission
- Contact your supervisor to add your email to the authorized users list
- Make sure the Google Sheet is shared with your account

### App looks broken or text is too small
**Solution**:
- **Zoom in**: Pinch out with two fingers
- **Zoom out**: Pinch in with two fingers
- **Reset zoom**: Chrome menu → Settings → Accessibility → Text scaling
- Rotate your phone to landscape mode for wider view

### "Add to Home screen" option not showing
**Solution**:
- Make sure you're using **Google Chrome** (not other browsers)
- Update Chrome to the latest version (Play Store → Chrome → Update)
- Try using "Desktop site" mode:
  1. Chrome menu → ☑ Desktop site
  2. Then try adding to home screen again

### App is slow or not loading
**Solution**:
- Check your internet speed (WiFi recommended)
- Close other apps to free up memory
- Clear Chrome cache (see instructions above)
- Restart your phone
- Try switching between WiFi and mobile data

### Data not updating
**Solution**:
- Pull down from the top of the page to refresh
- Check if you're signed in (for Production/Packing apps)
- Verify your internet connection is stable
- Contact your supervisor if Google Sheets permissions changed

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: Do I need to download anything from Play Store?
**A**: No! These are web apps. Just open them in Chrome browser. No installation needed from Play Store.

### Q2: Can I use the app offline?
**A**: No, you need an active internet connection (WiFi or mobile data) because the apps sync with Google Sheets in real-time.

### Q3: Will the app work on any Android phone?
**A**: Yes! The apps work on any Android phone (version 5.0+) with Chrome browser. Recommended: Android 8.0 or newer.

### Q4: Do I need a Google account?
**A**:
- **Production App**: Yes (requires Google sign-in)
- **Packing App**: Yes (requires Google sign-in)
- **Inventory App**: No (read-only, no sign-in needed)

### Q5: Can I use it on tablet?
**A**: Yes! The apps work great on Android tablets. Follow the same instructions.

### Q6: How do I update the app?
**A**: Web apps update automatically. Just refresh the page (swipe down from top) to get the latest version.

### Q7: Is my data safe?
**A**: Yes! All data is stored securely in Google Sheets with restricted access. Only authorized Google accounts can write data.

### Q8: What if I forget the app URL?
**A**: Bookmark it or add to home screen. You can also ask your supervisor or check your email/messages for the shared link.

### Q9: Can I use multiple apps at the same time?
**A**: Yes! You can open multiple apps in different Chrome tabs or add each app to your home screen.

### Q10: The sign-in keeps expiring. Why?
**A**: For security, Google OAuth tokens expire after 1 hour. Just sign in again when prompted.
